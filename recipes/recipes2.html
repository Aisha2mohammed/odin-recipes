Spaghetti Aglio e Olio:

The following are the ingredients i used .

Ingredient

  • 200 grams of spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust according to tastli)
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic turns golden and fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss well to coat the pasta with the garlic-infused oil.
  4. Season with salt according to taste.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese (optional) and chopped fresh parsley.
