chicken biryani

the following are the ingredient we used.

ingridient

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder

Instrucion

  1. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they are softened.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Pour enough water into the pot, about 4 cups, so that the rice is submerged. Bring it to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and let it cook for about 20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the flavors are well blended.
  4. Wash the rice and soak it in water for about 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a large pot, heat some ghee or oil. Add the sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside for garnishing.
  6. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with the reserved fried onions. Serve hot.
